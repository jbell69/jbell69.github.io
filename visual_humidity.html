Humidity

The humidity appears to be higher in the northern hemisphere then the southern. This may be due to the time these data points were taken (January). With January being the southern hemispheres "summertime" there is probably less moisture in the air from higher temperatures (on average). We would need to do proper statistics to verify if the higher humidity in near the north pole is significant.
