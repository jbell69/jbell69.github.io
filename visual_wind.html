Wind Speed

This chart show that the wind speed near the north pole are higher then anywhere else on the planet. This may be due to the time of year, January is winter for northern hemisphere and summer for southern hemisphere. The more consistant temperatures in the southern hemisphere may account for the lower wind speed. The same arguement could be made for the great temperature variations in the northern hemisphere affecting the increased wind speeds.
